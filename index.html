About

Xijun is a senior researcher of Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab. Before that, he has received M.Sc. degree under the supervision of Prof. Jianguo Yao (the Dean of the School of Software) from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2018. He is working towards his Ph.D. degree in the University of Science and Technology of China (HUAWEI-USTC Joint Ph.D. Program) under the supervision of Prof. Jie Wang (the Vice Dean of the School of the Gifted Young). He has published many papers on top peer-reviewed conferences and journals (such as NeurIPS, ICLR, KDD, ICDE, SIGMOD, DAC, etc.) and applied/published 10+ patents with Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. He has also won the championship of student learderboard in Dual Track of NeurIPS’21 ML4CO Competition. His recent research interests focus on Mathematical Programming Solver, Learning to Optimization (L2O), and Large Language Model for Optimization/Reasoning. Last but not least, he is one of core developers of the Huawei Cloud OptVerse AI Solver.
